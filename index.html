Key PET4 Findings:

• PET4-negative patients show 38 percentage points higher PFS (60% vs 22%)

• PET4-negative patients show 45 percentage points higher OS (85% vs 40%)

• PET4 status is strongly prognostic regardless of treatment arm

• PET4 can guide post-treatment monitoring and consolidation decisions
